About Us

Welcome to Gemma’s Hire Service, a family owned and operated hire company based in Harare, Zimbabwe. Having served for over 10 years, Gemma’s is proud to be the leader in the event industry.

Known throughout Zimbabwe for our amazing selection, great customer service, and can-do attitude, Gemma’s has the rental equipment to make your event spectacular.

Every event is enhanced by specialty rental items, knowledgeable experienced staff, and professional service. Whether a wedding in Victoria Falls, a corporate cocktail party at one of our many local venues, or an intimate dinner on the patio; Gemma’s Events can handle all your rental needs. It’s no wonder more people have been calling on Gemma’s Hire Service for over 10 years than any other party rental company to make their affair an unforgettable event.

Our Promise

When it's party time in the Zimbabwe - from Leopard Rock to Victoria Falls - Gemma’s Hire Service has you covered from tent top to tabletop. Our event professionals are experts at making sure every fork, knife, table, and chair is in top condition, delivered on time, and perfectly placed to your liking.

And our deep inventory of rental equipment ensures we have just the right supplies for your wedding, corporate event, or other affaire. So, whether you have the family over for the holidays, or are throwing a red carpet themed party, at Gemma’s Hire Service, we've got you covered.
